---
name: 6. send-email-administration
description: "Send an email to Sourcelabs administration with the work slips and travel costs of the previous month attached."
---

Use the M365 MCP server to send an email with the following details:

- **From:** s.pozzi@sourcelabs.nl
- **To:** s.pozzi@sourcelabs.nl
- **Subject:** Werkbriefjes en reiskosten {vorige-maand}
- **Body:** Hoi Lonneke, hierbij mijn werkbriefjes en reiskosten van de maand {vorige-maand}
- **Attachments:** all files from `Downloads/{previous_month-previous_year}` in `MM-YY` format (e.g. `Downloads/04-26` for April 2026)

Ask for confirmation before sending.
