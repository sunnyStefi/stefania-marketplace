# Monthly Tasks Orchestrator

Automatically run all three monthly tasks in sequence. The download paths use the format `MM-YY` (zero-padded month, two-digit year) and always target the **previous** month.

## Commands to execute (in order)

1. `/worksheet:monthly-tasks:4. download-hours-eneco`
   - Logs into Eneco Fieldglass
   - Downloads previous month's timesheets
   - Saves to `Downloads/{previous_month-previous_year}` (e.g. `Downloads/04-26`).

2. `/worksheet:monthly-tasks:5. download-ns-reishistorie`
   - Logs into ns.nl
   - Downloads previous month's travel costs
   - Saves to `Downloads/{previous_month-previous_year}` (e.g. `Downloads/04-26`).

3. `/worksheet:monthly-tasks:6. send-email-administration`
   - Composes email to Sourcelabs administration
   - Attaches work slips and travel costs from `Downloads/{previous_month-previous_year}` (e.g. `Downloads/04-26`).
   - **Asks for confirmation before sending.**

## Schedule

Run **first Monday of the month at start of day**.

Steps 1 and 2 run without pausing. Step 3 pauses once to confirm the email before sending.
