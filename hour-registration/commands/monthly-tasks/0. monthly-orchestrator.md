# Monthly Tasks Orchestrator

Automatically run all three monthly tasks in sequence. The download paths use the format `MM-YY` (zero-padded month, two-digit year) and always target the **previous** month (interpreted in the **Europe/Amsterdam** timezone).

## Commands to execute (in order)

1. `/hour-registration:monthly-tasks:4. download-hours-eneco`
   - Logs into Eneco Fieldglass
   - Downloads previous month's timesheets
   - Saves to `Downloads/{previous_month-previous_year}` (e.g. `Downloads/04-26`).

2. `/hour-registration:monthly-tasks:5. download-ns-reishistorie`
   - Logs into ns.nl
   - Downloads previous month's travel costs
   - Saves to `Downloads/{previous_month-previous_year}` (e.g. `Downloads/04-26`).

3. `/hour-registration:monthly-tasks:6. send-email-administration`
   - Composes email to Sourcelabs administration
   - Attaches work slips and travel costs from `Downloads/{previous_month-previous_year}` (e.g. `Downloads/04-26`).
   - **Asks for confirmation before sending.**
   - Cleans up `Downloads/{MM-YY}/` after the email is sent.

## Schedule

Run **first Monday of the month at start of day** (08:00).

Steps 4 and 5 run without pausing. Step 6 pauses once to confirm the email before sending.

## Error handling

If any step fails:

1. **Stop immediately** — do not run subsequent steps.
2. **Report clearly**: which step failed, what the error was, and what was (or was not) saved to `Downloads/{MM-YY}/`.
3. **How to resume**: fix the reported issue and re-run only the failed step. Steps 4 and 5 are safe to re-run (they overwrite any partially downloaded files). Step 6 checks that the download directory is non-empty before sending.

Transient failures (network timeout, page load error): the individual step retries up to **2 times** with a 30-second wait before reporting failure.
